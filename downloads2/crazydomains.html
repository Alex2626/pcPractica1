Learn about Domain Names

Domain Names have endless possibilities.

This is why you want to register a domain name with the best domain name registrar. A good domain name is something that's eye catching, easy to remember and rolls off the tongue when people talk about you.

Have you ever listened to the radio or seen a TV ad where companies advertise their website but a week later you can't remember it? It's because their domain name either isnt catchy enough or is too hard to remember.

Register your domain name today and create your website at Crazy Domains.

We provide thousands of Global domain names where you can register your domain including .com, .COM, .ORG, .CO, .NET, .NZ, .IN, .UK and much more.

Domain registration can be confusing and sometimes you don't know where to start.

We are the first choice for all Global domain names and we cannot be beaten. Simply head to Crazy Domains and search for the domain name you wish to register and select your extension (e.g. .COM, .com, .NET, .ORG, .CO, .IN, .NZ, .UK).

If you're confused about your extension, try these helpful tips; .COM is by far the most popular for worldwide options and general domain registration. If you are a Not-for-Profit, NGO, or a charity, try using .ORG short for organisation, these are commonly associated with organisations that dont operate for the profit or gain of individual members which translates to those viewing your website.

There are options for country code TLDs, too: such as .com, the most commonly used businesses.

A world of new generic or gTLDs is now also available to you, so you can register your .XYZ domain, as well as .CLUB, .LONDON, .MELBOURNE, .WEBSITE, .GURU and hundreds of others.

Once you have decided on what is right for your website you can proceed to our easy checkout service. After payment your domain will be instantly activated then you're done and ready to go. It's crazy easy!

What if the domain name you want is taken, you ask? Backorder it and well tell you the minute it becomes available. It couldnt be easier!

At Crazy Domains we pride ourselves on being the easiest to use and best value registrar in the business. Register your domain now!
